  2. Upload your HTML file (the one I made for you).

✅ Step 4: Deploy with GitHub Pages

  1. Go to Settings (on the repo, not your profile).

  2. Scroll down to Pages or click “Pages” in the left sidebar.

  3. Under “Source”, choose:

    • Branch: main

    • Folder: / (root)

  4. Click Save.

  5. Wait a few seconds — GitHub will show you the link to your live site (e.g. https://yourusername.github.io/savories-website).
